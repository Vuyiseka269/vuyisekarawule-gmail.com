Academics

Mahanyaneng Junior Secondary School is a Mathematics and Science Focus School. This means that our schools main
focus is on attaining and striving for excellence in Pure Mathematics and Physical Science. However, the school
also offers learners the choice of doing other main stream subjects (FET), as well as Arts related subjects

It is hard to believe that the First quarter of 2019 is nearly at an end! Learners should be preparing for
the upcoming Control Test assessments. These test in conjunction with other school based assessment tasks
form the mark for the first quarter. It is a good precursor of what can be expected in the June report.
