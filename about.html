About our School

The principal, educators, governing body and parents of Mahanyaneng Junior Secondary School strive to instil those morals and values
into their learners by developing their minds and bodies into becoming independent learners. It consists of the following:

Teaching by professional educators of the highest quality.
A balance between spiritual, scholastic, cultural and sport activities.
A healthy and active parent teacher relationship.
A relaxed but orderly learning environment in which teaching can occur.
Maintaining the rights of learners, educators and parents

Our Vision

The staff and school governing body of
Mahanyaneng Junior Secondary School
envisage the following:

To create a warm supportive atmosphere
where every child's positive image is
nurture;

To provide our learners with all-round
quality education in the classroom, on
the sports field and in the cultural
domain;

To encourage character traits as honesty,
responsibility, perseverance, care,
kindness towards others.

To instil an altitudinal disposition of
respect and appreciation of cultures
other than their own

Our Mission

Herewith commit the school to excellence in
education for all its learners.

This will be achieved by the following:

Fostering cooperation and sound interpersonal
relationships amongst all stakeholders.

Cultivating a climate of democratic openness,
involvement, sharing of information and
collective responsibility

Creating a sustainable environment by
providing the necessary educational
resources

Future orientation and holistic preparation of all
our learners to take up their rightful positions as
responsible citizens of our society.
